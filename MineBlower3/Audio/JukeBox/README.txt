Background music tracks used by the background music CrossFade object
These tunes are loopable excerpts from outsider music masterpieces.
The xFade() method crossfades between the currently playing track and
a new one.
In addition to reading the actual tunes from the mp3 files listed below,
the file OutsideGains.txt contains gain levels for the 5 tunes, one per
line in same order as the files.

The 5 tracks and their uses:
Outside0.mp3 - Jail House Rock by Eilert Pilarm (instruction screen)
Outside1.mp3 - The Touchy by Luie Luie (Main background music)
Outside2.mp3 - Stout Hearted Men by Shooby Taylor (Win music)
Outside3.mp3 - They Told Me by Jandek (Lose music)
Outside4.mp3 - 10 seconds of silence (for dialing out background music)
